2Ô∏è‚É£ GET vs POST Methods
üîπ GET Method

Data is visible in the URL

Used for search or filters

Less secure

Example URL:

üîπ POST Method

Data is hidden (sent in request body)

More secure

Used for login, registration, forms

Access data in PHP:
